Breachers

Free For All Deathmatch

Plant And Defuse


Early Development - Deathmatch With Friends


Breachers is a multiplayer FPS game made using Unreal Engine. Till now, there's a deathmatch mode, and there's a plant and defuse mode that's in progress. While it's very similar to Counter Strike, there are a few new ideas in mind. For example, the bomb will not spawn with the attackers. Instead, it'll spawn somewhere random in the map, and both the attackers and defenders will look for it, while dealing with each other. The defenders will also have the ability to take the bomb and possibly hide it from the attackers. This will create new gameplay dynamic and tactics. It'll be very interesting to see how the interactions will turn out. There are many more ideas like these ones.

Recent Work

Super Canary

The project I'm working on in my internship. It's about a Canary that is trying to rescue miners from beasts in the cave. Added some weapons and mechanics, but there's a lot more to do. Can't post repository link due to copy rights.

Foxy Kingdom

It's a 2D platformer about a fox that is trying to collect cherries to feed his family. He encounters wild animals that want to steal his food. The poor fox just wants to go home!

Space Frogger

This is a 2D procedurally generated endless runner. It's about a post apocalyptic world due to nuclear war that happens in the future. It mutates people into reptiles. It's also ruled killer robots. Our hero is trying to reach the time machine at the end of the platform to go back in time and stop the war. Little does he know, he's in an infinite loop made by the same time machine.

Village Defenders

A 2D top down tower defense game, where an army of the dead is sent by the dark lord to conquer the village. Enemies get harder to kill as the waves increase.

Laser Defender

After uncovering the plans of an alien race to use the newly created space warp technology to rule the galaxy, an earthling spaceship captain stole the device to bring it back to earth, only to be chased by the alien army.

NeuroEvolution Flappy Bird

Because I was bad at playing Flappy Bird, I made one for which the birds learn to play on their own using NeuroEvolution, which is a mix between Genetic Algorithm and Deep Neural Networks.

AR Demo

This isn't a game per se. It was an AR assessment for a job. And it was my first ever experience with AR. The idea was to have 3 different images to track, and each image had its unique set of characters that the user can scroll through. And when you tap the character, an UI menu appears that show info about them and buttons to control them.
